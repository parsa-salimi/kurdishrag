WELCOME

This is a place to find music you might not have heard yet — and, when it earns the space, books worth sitting with. Everything on this site is a recommendation. If a record or a book isn't here, it's because I didn't think it was worth your time.

Start with the music page for individual album reviews, or jump to the lists if you want curated starting points — gateway records for a genre, all-time favourites, what I have been listening to this month. Books are there too, alongside longer notes on artists and scenes worth exploring.

Ratings are out of 10 and reflect one person's ear. Use them as a rough signal — then go listen for yourself.
